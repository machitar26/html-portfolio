The Best anime According to Erwin

My top 3 anime of all-time.

One Piece

A long-running manga and anime series by Eiichiro Oda. It follows Monkey D. Luffy, a boy with a rubber body after eating a Devil Fruit, as he sails with his crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, in search of the legendary One Piece treasure. His dream is to become the Pirate King, and along the way, they face powerful enemies, uncover hidden secrets, and form deep friendships. The series is known for its epic world-building, emotional storytelling, and adventurous spirit.

One Punch Man

A superhero manga and anime created by ONE. It follows Saitama, a hero who can defeat any opponent with a single punch. Bored by his overwhelming strength, he seeks a true challenge while navigating the world of heroes, monsters, and the Hero Association. The series blends action, comedy, and satire of the superhero genre.

Dandadan

A supernatural manga series by Yukinobu Tatsu, blending action, comedy, and romance. It follows Okarun and Momo, two high schoolers who encounter both aliens and ghosts, gaining mysterious powers in the process. Together, they face bizarre supernatural threats while developing their bond.
